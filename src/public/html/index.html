About this tool

This tool is developed by a team of 2nd semester Computer Science students located at Aalborg University.
The tool has been developed as a product for the 2nd semester project in spring 2024.

The application is expected to act as a tool for improving team cohesion within a team of Computer Science students at Aalborg University.
